SowMate Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 May 2026

SowMate is designed to keep your garden planning information on your device.

Information Stored on Your Device

The app stores your selected planting region, liked vegetables, reminder frequency, notification setting, colour mode, and Harvest Log entries on your device.

Location

If you tap Use my location, SowMate asks for your current location to suggest an Australian planting region. Your location is not stored by the app after the suggestion is made.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, SowMate schedules local notifications for liked vegetables. You can change reminder frequency in the app and revoke notification permission in iPhone Settings.

Sharing and Third Parties

SowMate does not sell, share, or send your garden data to third parties. The app currently does not use accounts, advertising, analytics, or cloud sync.

Retention and Deletion

Data remains on your device until you delete it, change your settings, or delete the app. Deleting the app removes locally stored SowMate data from that device.

Your Choices

You can change planting region manually, turn reminders off, revoke location access, and revoke notification access in iPhone Settings.
